Depression is a mood disorder that can cause feelings of sadness, loss, or anger that interfere with everyday activities. Here are some ways to help cope with depression:

  • Lifestyle changes
    • Try to maintain a routine, including regular eating and sleeping habits, and exercise regularly. You can try a 15–30 minute walk, dancing, yoga, or stretching. You can also try to eat healthy foods and drink plenty of water.
  • Social support
    • Stay connected with friends and family, and talk through problems with a caring friend.
  • Express yourself
    • Try to do things that get your creative juices flowing, like reading a good book, watching a funny movie, or listening to music.
